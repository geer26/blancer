            </p>
            <p class="left-align">
                <a style="font-weight:bold; color: black;">1-a. - Login</a>
                <br><img src="/static/img/help/loginbutton.jpg" draggable="false" style="max-width: 25vw; max-height: 25vh;">
                <br>You can find the login button at the top left corner of the mainpage. The button appears only if you are logged out.
                <br>After you clicked, you have to provide your login credintentials (username and password respectively).
                <br>At checking the "remember me", next time you open your browser, you will be automatically logged in.
                <br><img src="/static/img/help/loginmodal.jpg" draggable="false" style="max-width: 25vw; max-height: 25vh;">
                <br>After you set all your creditentials, click on the LOGIN, and the application will log you in.
            </p>
            <p class="left-align">
                <a style="font-weight:bold; color: black;">1-b. - Logout</a>
                <br><img src="/static/img/help/pwreset.jpg" draggable="false" style="max-width: 25vw; max-height: 25vh;">
                <br>You can find the logout button at the top left corner of the mainpage. On click the application will immediatelly log you out.
                <br>The logout button appears only if you are logged in.
            </p>
            <p class="left-align">
                <a style="font-weight:bold; color: black;">1-c. - Password reset</a>
                <br>You can find the password reset button at the top left corner of the mainpage next to the logout button.
                <br>On click the application will ask you for the current password and the new password twice (to avoid mispelling).
                <br><img src="/static/img/help/pwresetmodal.jpg" draggable="false" style="max-width: 25vw; max-height: 25vh;">
                <br>After clicking on RESET PASSWORD and no error occurs, your password has been changed, that you have to use at the next login.
                <br>The password reset button appears only if you are logged in.
            </p>
            <p class="left-align">
                <a style="font-weight:bold; color: black;">1-d. - Forgotten password</a>
                <br>You can find the forgotten password on the login panel. After you clicked, READ and FOLLOW the instructions you get.
                <br>In lack of valid email address you won't be able to reset your password.
                <br>For sake of your privacy, you won't get any error or success message. Do not worry, the application does his job. Check your email.
            </p>
