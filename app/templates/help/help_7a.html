Pocket details

The details of the selected pocket can be displayed. The details includes the charts and the transfers.

The application provides 3 charts: one for all incomes in the selected date range, one for all expenses in the selected date range and one for the whole money-flow in in the selected date range.
The examined daterange can be changed on the top of the panel. The starting date will start at the selected date at 00:00, the ending date will end at the selected date 23:59.
The transfers in the selected date range can be displayed at clicking on "SHOW TRANSFERS". The transfers will be displayed ordered by timestamp increasingly.

In transfers view at clicking at the "SHOW CHARTS" on the top of the list, the chart view can be displayed again.
