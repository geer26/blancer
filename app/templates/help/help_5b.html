Add transfer

By clicking on the publish or on the publish icon, the user can register a transfer.

The default pocket where the transfer belong to, is the selected pocket, but it can changed.
The transfer category is the first transfer in alphabetically order, and it can be changed also easily.
The transfer amount must be numeric.
The Blancer does not handles floats or decimals, the amount must be integer value, or the transfer won"t be registered!
The user has the opportunity to attach detailed description to a transfer also, default there are no description.
After adding a transfer, there is no opportunity to undo it, the balance of the pocket will be recalculated.
By deleting transfer category, the transfer itself will be showed in the charts.
