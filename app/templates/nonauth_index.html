Have You ever been in a situation at the end of a month: "Where the hell did all my salary go? I do not even know what I spent for..." If not, you are in the luckiest 1%.

Taking care of your financial health is as important to your personal growth as is taking care of your physical health. A chaotic financial life reflects a chaotic life, period.

Maybe You realized tracking your money flow can help you spend less and save more. And maybe you hunching over tons of bills and notes on evenings at your desk registering precisely all your expenses and incomes in that day.

Tracking Your money flow itself wont't help you to reach the Financial Consciousness. It is You who can help Yourself by analyzing the expenses and incomes, find the wastings and the weak points.

Long story short, if you want to climb the pyramid of Financial Consciousness, you have to start track your money flow as first step.
