Solution

The Blancer© can answer all these challenges. It is designed and developed friendliness, speed, usability, accessibility, security, and Your goals kept in mind

The Blancer© manages a secure database and you manage the application. You can create and modify budgets, money-flow classes, you can register expenses or incomes instantly on-the-spot, you can analyze your money flow in a period defined by you.

You can forget the almost-impossible-to-find notes and the complicated lists of expenses and incomes, the incovenient counting over and over again, the uncomfortable updating your budget's actual balance. You can leave all this to Balncer©.

Just plan your budget (the Blancer© calls the budgets Pockets), define your money-flow elements (called for Transfers), and register your expenses and incomes. The Balncer© will take the work, and you will be instantly informed on your financial habits and status.

The Blancer© also provides you a detailed and decent help so if you get stuck, you can easily get out.
